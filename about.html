About Benkees

Benkees is a fictional team created by Apex Angel.
Each character represents a different personality and emotion.



Sakura

A cheerful and kind-hearted presence. Represents warmth and innocence.

John

Calm and realistic. Represents balance and quiet thinking.

Clara

Intellectual and reserved. Represents knowledge and introspection.

Harry

Rebellious and sharp. Represents anger and resistance.

Jimmy

Energetic and chaotic. Represents impulse and instinct.

Tanaka

Ordinary and grounded. Represents everyday life.

Poko

Mysterious and silent. Represents the unknown.

Isabel

Elegant and dominant. Represents pride and control.

Buster

Aggressive and bold. Represents raw power and instinct.
