About Benkees

Benkees is a fictional team created by Apex Angel.
Each character represents a different personality and emotion.

Sakura

Cheerful and kind-hearted.
Represents warmth and innocence.

John

Calm and realistic.
Represents balance and quiet thinking.

Clara

Intellectual and thoughtful.
Observes deeply.

Harry

Rebellious and sharp.
Questions everything.

Jimmy

Wild and emotional.
Represents chaos and instinct.

Tanaka

Serious and disciplined.
Represents structure.

Poko

Mysterious and silent.
Exists beyond logic.

Isabel

Elegant and dominant.
Represents control and pride.

Buster

Rough and aggressive.
Represents raw power.
