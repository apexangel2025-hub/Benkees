About Benkees

Benkees is a strange group of characters living somewhere around "North Station".
They may look unusual, but each of them quietly lives their own ordinary life.

Sakura

A normal high school girl.
Always smiling, kind, and gentle.

John

Sakura’s childhood friend.
Serious, but only speaks in realistic terms.

Clara

A university student living near Sakura.
Loves literature and quiet moments.

Harry

An unfriendly man inspired by Dirty Harry.
Seems cold, but actually kind at heart.

Jimmy

A music-loving guy from the neighborhood.
Secretly prefers classical over rock.

Tanaka

A middle-aged office worker.
Often says, "Things were better back then."

Poko

Sakura’s childhood friend.
Almost silent—until danger appears.

Isabel

Almost like a villainess.
Polite but sharp-tongued, yet shy and kind inside.

Buster

Rumored to be a "hitman" or "sniper".
In reality, just a professional pest control worker—perfect at his job.
